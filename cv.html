The summary

A product designer with 8+ years turning complex, regulated systems into experiences that feel effortless. At Digidentity I'm the sole designer across iOS, Android, and web — balancing security and compliance with UX everyday people can actually use, and owning the design system that ties it together. A background in illustration and motion gives me an edge in interaction design and storytelling: I care as much about how something feels as whether it works. I thrive in collaborative teams where design has genuine influence, and I'm drawn to products that earn people's trust and make a real difference in their lives.

Experience, in depth

Product Designer · Digidentity
Feb 2023 – Present · The Hague
  • Digidentity is a Qualified Trust Service Provider on the EU Trusted List (eIDAS), whose platform has created 25M+ digital identities across 180+ nationalities for 250K+ corporate customers — I'm the sole designer for the products that run on it.
  • Sole product designer across native mobile (iOS & Android) and web, owning end-to-end UX for identity verification, account recovery, and digital signature flows.
  • Led the account recovery redesign across 5 distinct user scenarios and 8 languages, improving accessibility and reducing user drop-off.
  • Consolidated a multi-PIN system into a single PIN per account for 300K+ users, while rebranding the product's core terminology.
  • Designed and maintained a scalable design system used across web and mobile, enabling faster engineering handoff and consistent UI.
  • Collaborated cross-functionally with security, compliance, engineering, and QA to ship features meeting eIDAS regulatory requirements without compromising usability.
  • Treated accessibility (WCAG 2.1 AA) and localisation as non-negotiable design constraints across every initiative.
Product & Digital Designer · Sushara Studio
2018 – 2022 · Independent practice

Ran an independent design practice spanning product and brand: rebranded and built a responsive site for Shiva Yoga Center using design-thinking methodology, created brand identities and websites for startups (Remasterdam, Bao Khao, Voltron, Zorg Van Toen), and led illustration and marketing collateral for clients including Puuro Earth and Juizs.

Social Media Designer · Jimmy Nelson Studio
