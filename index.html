How I Work

I work at the intersection of trust and usability — turning complex problems into experiences that feel clear, effortless, and genuinely usable for everyone.

As the sole designer on complex, high-stakes products, I lead through collaboration — working directly with PMs, engineers, security and compliance, the service desk, and QA to make the right trade-offs between hard constraints and the user experience.

I believe the best design comes from understanding constraints deeply enough to design within them gracefully, rather than fighting against them — grounded in research, testing, and iteration. I own the design system, ship across three platforms, and write copy that survives translation into eight languages.
