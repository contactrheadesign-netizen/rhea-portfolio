How I Work

I work at the intersection of trust and usability — designing experiences where getting it wrong means someone can't sign a contract, file taxes, or prove their identity.

As the sole designer on regulated products, I lead through collaboration — working directly with PMs, engineers, security and compliance teams, the service desk, and QA to make the right trade-offs between compliance requirements and user experience.

I believe the best design work in regulated spaces comes from understanding constraints deeply enough to design within them gracefully, rather than fighting against them. I own the design system, ship across three platforms, and write copy that survives translation into eight languages.
